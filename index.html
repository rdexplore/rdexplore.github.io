About Me

LinkedIn

I'm an AI strategist and builder with 13+ years of global consulting experience advising enterprises in strategy and AI activation roadmaps, while independently building across the applied AI stack: multi-agent validators, multi-model intelligence pipelines, MCP-based workflow automations, and deploying local LLM models via open source models platforms such as Ollama and Hugging Face.
